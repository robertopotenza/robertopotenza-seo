Fundação Getulio Vargas

About FGV

A prestigious Brazilian institution renowned for excellence

Fundação Getulio Vargas (FGV) is a prestigious Brazilian institution renowned for its excellence in education, research, and public policy. Established in 1944, FGV has consistently ranked among the top institutions in Brazil, Latin America, and globally.

As an alumnus of FGV, I gained valuable knowledge and skills that have been instrumental in my career as a manufacturing and operations executive. The institution's rigorous academic programs, world-class faculty, and emphasis on practical application provided me with a strong foundation for leadership in global industrial environments.
